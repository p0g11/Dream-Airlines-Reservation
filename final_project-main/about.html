ABOUT US

Dream Airlines Company (DAC) began working in the airline sector in March 2001 and invented the "cheap fare, great value" business model. Since its beginning, it has carried more than 180 million passengers. One of the most up-to-date aircraft fleets in the world, DAC now runs a fleet of 48 Airbus (seven A321neo, seven A321ceo, five A320neo, 26 A320, seven A330, and one A330 freighter) and 21 ATR (six ATR 72-500, thirteen ATR 72-600, and two ATR Freighters). Dream Airlines will take delivery of 26 further Airbus A321neo, three additional ATR 72-600, 16 additional A330neo, and fifteen additional A320 family aircraft between 2021 and 2028. In addition to its 25 foreign destinations, CEB's 36 local destinations build a vast network throughout the Philippines. Manila, Cebu, Clark, Kalibo, Iloilo, Davao, and Cagayan de Oro are the organization's seven strategically located centers. The airline continues to devise creative pricing strategies so it may constantly offer low year-round fares for all its flights. As it goes all out in its customer-first initiatives, Dream Airlines Company (DAC) is among the first airlines in Asia to invest in an integrated facility and technology for social intelligence and customer engagement with its Customer Command Center.

The 24/7 command center is equipped with social monitoring, publishing and engagement tools—all tasked to assist travelers on their concerns and inquiries. In the Philippines, DAC is also the go-to airline for air cargo, connecting islands through trade in goods. It serves a wide network, including individual shippers and cargo agencies domestically and abroad, by offering competitive, quick, flexible, and uncomplicated air cargo service. Moreover, CEB will be the only passenger airline in the Philippines with a fleet of aircraft solely dedicated to carrying cargo because it anticipates receiving its converted freighter aircraft within the next year. Customers have come to expect a particularly positive flying experience with DAC because this airline is the only one that provides entertainment in the air with its "Fun Games" on board and its popular, well-known inflight magazine, Smile. Through the "Change for Good" project, passengers can also assist Filipino children by donating spare change while in flight. In order to ensure that everyone may travel safely and responsibly, CEB continues to apply a multi-layered approach to safety in accordance with international aviation regulations, earning it a COVID-19 compliance rating of 7/7 stars from Airline Ratings. These include antigen testing for DAC frontliners prior to duty, contactless procedures, extensive cleaning and disinfection processes for all aircraft and facilities, and required mask and face shield use for both passengers and personnel. In addition to this, jet aircraft have 99.99% effective hospital-grade HEPA filters to prevent the spread of viruses.

Our purposes

To provide affordable travel for passengers by flying them in and out of several cities around the world.

Our airline provides a variety of services to its customers, such as ticketing and check-in, ground services, flight operations, and information about local airports. The experienced members of the crew will collaborate with the passengers to make sure that the trip goes off without a hitch and is done as quickly and effectively as possible. The user will next be presented with the airline reservation form. This is the form that the user will fill out to book their ticket to whatever location they choose to travel to. Additionally, it includes the user's personal information in order to prevent scams.
